What is New World?

New World is an open world MMORPG in which you play a custom character of your own making. This character uses various weapons, items and abilities to battle forces of nature and Corruption. This "Corruption" is the main anatagonist in the game and is gradually taking control of all living things in the natural world.

You begin your journey on a ship at sea. soon in the prologue the ship is sunk by a major storm and you are left stranded on a beach to fend for yourself. find matirials to craft items nessessary for survival and best of luck in the brave New World!
